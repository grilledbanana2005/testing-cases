Tên Sản phẩm

1.000.000 ₫

This flowering bonsai produces blooms during the spring and possibly during the fall with the proper care. Its blush (pink) blooms can add a refreshing touch to a room. The bonsai's soft green leaves are contrasted by the blooms.
